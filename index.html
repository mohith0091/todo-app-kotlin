Privacy Policy for One UI Todo

Effective Date: 5 Aug, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to One UI Todo! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our app. Since One UI Todo is designed to be a simple todo management app and does not collect any personal data, this policy is brief.

2. Data Collection and Usage

One UI Todo does not collect, store, or share any personal data or information about you. The app operates solely on your device, and no data is transmitted to us or any third parties.

3. Data Security

As One UI Todo does not handle any personal data, there are no specific security measures for protecting data in this context. However, we strive to ensure that the app itself functions securely and reliably.

4. Third-Party Services

One UI Todo does not use any third-party services that collect, store, or process personal data. The app does not include analytics, advertisements, or external services that could impact your privacy.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at developers.iconic@gmail.com.
